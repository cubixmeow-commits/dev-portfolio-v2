# Cookbook from public knowledge: Write an Email That Gets Answered

## Category chosen

**Writing & Publishing** (`writing-publishing`)

### Why it fills a gap

After Study Plan filled Learning & Teaching, Writing & Publishing still had no Cookbook. Clear email is a universal beginner task: high demand, low prerequisites, and a natural fit for Product Law 002 (hide writing craft; ask only for known facts).

## Complexity Gate (Product Law 002)

| Check | Result |
| --- | --- |
| Audience fit | Pass: people who freeze at a blank email |
| Expertise demand | Pass: recipient, ask, facts, tone, sign-off only |
| Judgment visibility | Pass: prompts carry plain-language craft |
| Fact honesty | Pass: invent-nothing on motives, calendars, policies |
| Surface simplicity | Pass: three steps, ~28 minutes |
| Failure test | Pass: does not teach "business writing" as a profession |

Failed checks: **0**.

## Public sources used for inspiration (ideas only)

1. Digital.gov plain language principles (audience first; organize with the point up front; active voice; short sentences).
2. Federal Plain Language Guidelines family of public government writing advice.
3. Section 508 / accessibility-oriented public notes on clear subject lines and concise email purpose.

No guideline paragraphs, examples, or layouts were copied. The sample project (apartment heater repair) is fictional SousMeow content.

## How ideas were transformed

| Public concept | SousMeow Recipe |
| --- | --- |
| Write for your audience | Name the reader and the one ask |
| Put the important information first + keep it short | Write subject and short email |
| Revise and check understanding | Revise for clarity and pack a checklist |

## Reusable pattern reinforced

1. Empty category + everyday life job.
2. Public method family with open educational / government guidance.
3. Ban source wording; invent a concrete sample project.
4. Pantry = facts the sender already knows.
5. Teach retry advice inside `why_it_matters` and checks.
6. Keep under ~30 minutes when the outcome is a single artifact family (here: one email).
